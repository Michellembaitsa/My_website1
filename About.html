About me.

Michelle Mbaitsa is a beginner techie, learning how to code at AkiraChix,born and raised in Nairobi,Kenya.Michelle experienced life from the rougher parts of the city and had to quickly learn the importance of self values which she has held on to and have been her compass to finding good opportunities for herself. Resilience is her top value and has kept her focused on challenging herself to make the most out of her life.One of her favorite quotes is by Rosa Parks,"To bring out change, you must not be afraid to take the first step.We will fail when we fail to try. "

She is passionate about programming and her biggest achievement has been getting accepted to AkiraChix,a place where she is building her skills and joining others in the tech community to contribute towards coming up with innovative solutions to help solve for Africa and beyond.

If you do not find Michelle on a laptop screen working towards becoming a software developer,you can find her singing along to some alternative music, doing a DIY challenge or failing terribly at her rendition of some recipe she just watched.
